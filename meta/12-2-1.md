# Indicator definition and additional information

national_indicator_description: |-
  This indicator measures the share of establishments
  investing in environmental protection activities or management practices.
  <br><br>
  Environmental protection and resource management activities reflect the proportion of establishments investing in environmental protection activities. These are businesses that incurred expenditures on such activities during the reference year. This does not include expenditures made in previous years, even if the effects of those expenditures extend over several years.
  <br><br>
  Environmental management practices refer to the use of one or more environmental management practices.

computation_calculations: All of the percentages are calculated in relation to the
  total number of establishments in the category.

comments_limitations: |-
  Data by geographic region cannot be disaggregated by industry and conversely, data by industry cannot be disaggregated by geographic region.
  <br><br>
  The industries included in this indicator do not encompass all those in the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS), but focus primarily on those with the greatest environmental impacts.
  <br><br>
  ISO 14000 family is an internationally recognized set of environmental management standards and guidelines that are primarily concerned with environmental management systems developed by the International Organization for Standardization.
